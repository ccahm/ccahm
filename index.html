Welcome to CCA

The world of education is a highly interesting vast expanse to be explored. Its span is getting even wider with ample opportunities opening up for students.

I warmly welcome you to CCA whose mission is to cull students like you into IT professionals by imparting expert academic knowledge blended with practical training provided in the environment of the best of infrastructure and facilities.

CCA opportunities are sparse. We have started this Institutions for IT for students like you who aspire to make a promising career in this field. I trust that you, my dear friends, will put in your utmost to master these skills and contribute your bit to society by helping the healthcare industry to give people a healthy, quality life.

No company is ready to invest time to train a single graduate and make him suitable for a job. The onus is on the student to enhance his employability. In pursuance of the dreams only a few are aware of the challenges. Our curricula are designed not only to bring the students face to face with these challenges, but also to overcome them. Now, one needs industry specific knowledge and skill to make the cut. At CCA we help students develop these skills and help them to enhance their employability.
